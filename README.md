# Certification Study Coach

Status: In development.

A Claude‑powered, multi‑turn study assistant designed to make certification prep more interactive, adaptive, and personalized. This tool generates practice questions, explains concepts, and builds tailored study plans based on the learner’s goals and current level.

## Why It Exists
Most certification prep is passive — long videos, dense PDFs, and static question banks.  
Learners often don’t know what to focus on, how to pace themselves, or whether they’re actually improving.

This tool turns certification prep into an **active learning experience**.  
It adapts to the learner, surfaces weak areas, and provides real‑time feedback so studying becomes more efficient, more engaging, and more effective.

## What It Generates
- **Practice questions** with explanations  
- **Multi‑turn tutoring** on any topic  
- **Personalized study plans**  
- **Adaptive difficulty** based on performance  
- **Concept breakdowns** and targeted review  

## Features
- Claude API–generated quizzes and explanations  
- Topic‑based study sessions  
- Instant feedback loops  
- Adaptive difficulty and pacing  
- Support for multiple certification domains  

## Roadmap
- Progress tracking and learning analytics  
- Flashcard mode with spaced repetition  
- Full exam simulation mode  
- Weak‑area detection and targeted remediation  
- Exportable study plans (PDF / Markdown)
